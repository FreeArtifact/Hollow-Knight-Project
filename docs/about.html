Extraction

Prev Next

Extracting the files was, unfortunately, not as simple as dragging the some files into a folder. What you get when you do something like that is the visual equivalent of a heat stroke (it's all HEX code). Fortunately, there exist solutions for this, one being Asset Ripper! This tool, meant for extracting game assets out of Unity projects like Hollow Knight, is what enabled me to easily retrieve whatever text was available.
